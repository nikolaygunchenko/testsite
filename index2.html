First Blog entry



Welcome to the GoSikh blog. This blog will have a few entries about what material to choose, what length of turban you might need and how best to take care of these Turbans and Patkas. 

We started in 2006 and have been selling Turbans online since then and somewhere along the way we also sell Patkas online. 

As usual comments are welcome, you can leave your comments here or you can email me directly.
